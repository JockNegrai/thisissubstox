FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial? Do I need a credit card?›
Yes — all plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only add payment details when you decide to subscribe.
What counts as a "location"?›
A location is a distinct physical warehouse, store, or fulfillment center. Multiple zones or bins within a single building count as one location. You can add unlimited zones and bins inside each location.
Can I import my existing inventory data?›
Absolutely. SubStox supports CSV import for SKUs, lots, purchase orders, and suppliers. We also provide an import template and a guided walkthrough so your data maps correctly on the first try.
Can I switch plans or cancel any time?›
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time — changes take effect immediately and are prorated. You can cancel before your trial ends with no charge. Annual plans receive a prorated credit toward the new plan.
How does the annual discount work?›
Toggle to Annual billing on the pricing page — prices drop 10% automatically. For Growth that's $134/mo instead of $149, saving you $180 per year. For Starter it's $62/mo instead of $69.
Does SubStox connect to QuickBooks or Xero?›
QuickBooks Online and Xero sync is available on the Enterprise plan. COGS entries, PO receipts, and inventory adjustments post automatically, keeping your books in sync without manual entry.
How accurate is the AI demand forecasting?›
Our models use 90+ days of sales history combined with seasonality detection and trend analysis. Customers typically see 15–30% improvement in forecast accuracy compared to spreadsheet-based methods. Forecasts improve over time as more data is collected.
Is my data secure?›
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS with daily automated backups. Enterprise plans include custom data residency options.
